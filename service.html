Crane Hire Services

We regularly carry out lifts for the construction sector, including lifting roof trusses, chimneys, spandrel panels, steels and concrete beams. Our crane hire services can include carrying out machinery movement on your behalf alongside a full range of lifts within the industrial and engineering sectors, including AHU lifts, plant and factory equipment lifts and container lifts. A-Lift has worked within the civil infrastructure sector, on road, rail and airport developments and undertaking bridge and wind turbine installations. We also frequently carry out the hot tub, pool and swim spa lifts and larger marine boat lifts for domestic customers and can assist in forestry and tree removal or lifting ornamental structures and ponds. The list is endless! We concentrate our services within the counties of Northamptonshire, Bedfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Cambridgeshire, so give us a call on our freephone number and we can happily advise if we are able to help with your crane hire needs in that area.
